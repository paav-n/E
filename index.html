    Our Policy: Privacy

    This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for enthalpylogistics.com. This privacy notice applies solely to information collected by this website. It will notify you of the following: What personally identifiable information is collected from you through the website, how it is used and with whom it may be shared. What choices are available to you regarding the use of your data. The security procedures in place to protect the misuse of your information. How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information by alerting Enthalpy customer services. Information Collection, Use, and Sharing We are the sole owners of the information collected on this site. We only have access to/collect information that you voluntarily give us via email or other direct contact from you. We will not sell or rent this information to any third party organization. We will use your information to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us. We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request, e.g. to ship an order. Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy. Your Access to and Control Over Information You may opt out of any future contacts from us at any time. You can do the following at any time by contacting us via the email address or phone number given on our website: See what data we have about you, if any. Change/correct any data we have about you. Have us delete any data we have about you. Express any concern you have about our use of your data. Security We take precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline. Wherever we collect sensitive information (such as credit card data), that information is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way. You can verify this by looking for a lock icon in the address bar and looking for "https" at the beginning of the address of the Web page. While we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online, we also protect your information offline. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, billing or customer service) are granted access to personally identifiable information. The computers/servers in which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment. Registration If you are planning to use Enthalpy to expand your venue’s catering services the venue must first complete the registration form. During registration a user is required to give certain information (such as name, email address, business address, menu options). This information is used to contact you about current and newly released products/services that Enthalpy will offer in which you have expressed interest in. At your option, you may also provide specific information (such as gender, age or ideal customer segments) about your business’ preferences, but it is not required. Our independently contracted drivers (“maestros”) will need to create a personal profile and register their vehicle on Enthalpy. Maestros will need to go through a background check which can be in person and with a submission of their resume to an Enthalpy representative. All information given by the maestros to Enthalpy will not be shared with any third parties to Enthalpy.     Our Policy: Return

    Thanks for making your catering order at enthalpylogistics.com. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.To be eligible for a return of an order that can possibly result in a full refund, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it and demand for a refund can be made thirty minutes has passed from the time the catering order was meant to be delivered by. Every order comes with a digital receipt so this will be used to verify the reference point at which refund demands can be made by customers making catering orders. Your item must be in the original packaging. Proof of purchase is stored online so we will have that on record. Applicable Incidents for Refund Payments If an order is late due to the maestro being immensely past the intended delivery time (30 minutes or more), than the customer have a full right to request and receive a refund for the order that they placed. If a maestro arrives at the requested time of pickup, but if the order is not prepared due to the inability of the caterer (“venue”) to have it prepared on time then the burden of the order’s delay in delivery lies on the venue. However, because Enthalpy Inc.’s underlying business model focuses on optimizing the performance of small business’ catering logistics we understand that unexpected circumstances can occur and will inhibit the performance of a venue at an unfavorable time. Therefore, Enthalpy will enact a forgiveness policy where the first order that is delivered 30 minutes late from the intended delivery time, due to unfortunate circumstances from the venues, will entitle the venue to a full refund of the Enthalpy commission placed on the first order where the fault lay with the venue. Once the venue claims this forgiveness refund, which a written explanation from the venue is required to substantiate the full refund, If the fault of the order lies on the venue and or the patron being late in responding about picking up the order at the designated location than the maestro will be paid as usual without any repercussions to his or her status as a maestro. However if it is made clear from Enthalpy’s systems that the maestro was not on time to pickup an order resulting in a late order for the patron, which both the patron and Enthalpy will note, than the Maestro will be at fault be penalized in compensation unless the maestro writes an appeal explaining why they were late for the pickup of the order to begin with. If Enthalpy customer services deems that the Maestro’s reasoning is valid, and leniency will be applied for first time offenses, than the maestro will be awarded the full compensation that Enthalpy guarantees every maestro as stated in it’s business models. Refunds Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after inspecting the item. If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your credit card (or the original method of payment). You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies. The flat delivery fee, that will be noted on both the checkout order form and the digital receipt, is non-refundable. Refunds (if applicable) Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If you are approved, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment, within a certain amount of days. Late or missing refunds (if applicable) If you haven't received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. Next contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted. If you've done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at . Contact Us If you have any questions on how to return your item to us or about the text above. Please contact Enthalpy Inc at help@enthalpylogistics.com or by calling either (201) 887-4181 or (201) 647-5679.
